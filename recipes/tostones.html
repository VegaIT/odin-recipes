Fried Tostones

Description

Fried tostones are a great, easy and quick snack or side dish. Great for dipping in either a mixture of mayo and ketchup or garlic and olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 2 Green Plantains
  • 1 cup Vegitable oil
  • Salt to taste

Recipe Instructions

  1. Pour oil into frying pan and set butner on medium high.
  2. While oil is heating, cut each end off of the Plantains. Slice down the skin of the plantain from end to end, then use thumbs to peel off skin.
  3. Cut about 1 inch slices of plantain slightly diagonal.
  4. Place slices in oil for a few minutes and flip once.
  5. Once the plantains start to brown remove from oil and place on paper towel.
  6. Use either two plates or plantain smasher to flatten the semi-cooked plantain.
  7. Continue frying flattened plantains for an additional 5 minutes or so, flipping one more time until desired crispyness.
  8. Add salt to taste.
